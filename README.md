# Desert-Command
Super Basic Work ts is a military game thats all.


<img width="1282" height="796" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/1839f507-c129-4ac3-8bf9-134e7399084b" />

As you can see, the platform consists of small squares. You can place as many troops as your "AP" allows on these squares.

You are initially given 500 AP, and you need to survive until the next wave and position your army well.

<img width="1279" height="800" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/56abf6b9-390c-4c86-b384-cf1ca810e46a" />

The moment you enter combat, randomly generated enemy units will appear.

You need to left-click on the unit you want to move or attack.

When you run out of moves or have arranged the unit as you wish, press the "TUR BİTİR" button and arrange it again.
